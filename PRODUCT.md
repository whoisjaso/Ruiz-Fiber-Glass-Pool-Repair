# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Houston-area homeowners and property owners who need practical fiberglass pool repair, refinishing, component work, or concrete overlay support. They are likely comparing local contractors on a phone and need fast confirmation that Ruiz Fiberglass Pool Repair handles their specific issue.

## Product Purpose

The website exists to turn local search and referral traffic into phone calls and estimate requests for fiberglass pool repair and new refinishing in Houston, TX and surrounding Harris County areas. Success means visitors quickly understand the service focus, see the phone number, identify the problem Ruiz can fix, and request an estimate.

## Brand Personality

Direct, local, contractor-grade, repair-focused, Texas-proud, clean, and trustworthy. The site should feel like a polished digital version of the company truck-door identity, not a resort pool builder template.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic luxury pool-builder styling, dark blue spa visuals, thin elegant type, script fonts, stock resort imagery, vague lifestyle copy, hidden phone numbers, and generic wave logos that replace the existing truck-door identity.

## Design Principles

1. Keep the phone number and estimate action visible at all times.
2. Preserve the work-truck identity: black arched lettering, Texas red, clean white, pool-blue accents, and fiberglass repair focus.
3. Explain repair services in plain contractor language, with no inflated reputation claims.
4. Make mobile scanning simple: problem, service, phone, form.
5. Use visual texture from fiberglass, concrete, pool water, dotted patterns, and red/black sweep lines without reducing readability.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for text and interactive controls. Support keyboard navigation, visible focus states, reduced motion preferences, readable mobile type, clear form labels, and non-color-only cues for required actions.
